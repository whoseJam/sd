对称的正方形

本题的关键在于如何提取二维矩阵的Hash值

$$ \begin{aligned} H_{n,m}&=\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m X^{n-i}Y^{m-j}c_{i,j} \\ &=XH_{n-1,m}+YH_{n,m-1}-XYH_{n-1,m-1} \end{aligned} $$

对称的正方形

$$ \begin{aligned} H_{x_1,y_1,x_2,y_2}&=H_{x_2,y_2} \\ &-X^{x_2-x_1}H_{x_1-1,y_2} \\ &-Y^{y_2-y_1}H_{x_2,y_1-1} \\ &+X^{x_2-x_1}Y^{y_2-y_1}H_{x_1-1,y_1-1} \end{aligned} $$

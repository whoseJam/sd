Crash的数字表格

给定 $n,m$，欲求 $$ \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m lcm(i,j) $$

Crash的数字表格

遇见 $lcm$，把它转为 $gcd$

Crash的数字表格

$$ \begin{aligned} \sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m lcm(i,j) &=\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m\frac{ij}{gcd(i,j)} \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\frac 1 d\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^m ij[gcd(i,j)=d] \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}\frac 1 d\sum_{i=1}^{n/d}\sum_{j=1}^{m/d}(id)(jd)[gcd(i,j)=1] \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum_{i=1}^{n/d}\sum_{j=1}^{m/d}ij\sum_{k|i\\k|j}\mu(k) \end{aligned} $$

Crash的数字表格

$$ \begin{aligned} answer&=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum_{i=1}^{n/d}\sum_{j=1}^{m/d}ij\sum_{k|i\\k|j}\mu(k) \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum_{k=1}^{min(n/d,m/d)}\mu(k)\sum_{1\le i\le n/d\\k|i}i\sum_{1\le j\le m/d\\k|j}j \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum_{k=1}^{min(n/d,m/d)}\mu(k)k^2S_1(1,\lfloor\frac{n}{kd}\rfloor)S_1(1,\lfloor\frac{m}{kd}\rfloor) \end{aligned} $$

Crash的数字表格

$$ \begin{aligned} answer&=\sum_{d=1}^{min(n,m)}d\sum_{k=1}^{min(n/d,m/d)}\mu(k)k^2S_1(1,\lfloor\frac{n}{kd}\rfloor)S_1(1,\lfloor\frac{m}{kd}\rfloor) \\ &=\sum_{T=1}^{min(n,m)}S_1(1,\lfloor\frac n T\rfloor)S_1(1,\lfloor\frac m T\rfloor)\sum_{k|T}\mu(k)k^2(\frac T k) \\ &=\sum_{T=1}^{min(n,m)}S_1(1,\lfloor\frac n T\rfloor)S_1(1,\lfloor\frac m T\rfloor)T\sum_{k|T}\mu(k)k \end{aligned} $$

设 $F(n)=\sum_{k|n}\mu(k)k$，这竟然是一个积性函数（当然即使不是积性函数也能 $O(nlogn)$ 算出，并且也能通过本题）

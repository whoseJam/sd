ZAP

$n$ 次询问，每次询问查询 $$ \sum_{x=1}^a\sum_{y=1}^b[gcd(x,y)=k] $$

ZAP

遇见 $[expr=k]$，就用 $\sum_{d|n}\mu(d)=[n=1]$ 去替换

ZAP

$$ \begin{aligned} \sum_{x=1}^a\sum_{y=1}^b[gcd(x,y)=k] &=\sum_{x=1}^a\sum_{y=1}^b[gcd(\frac x k,\frac y k)=1] \\ &=\sum_{x=1}^a\sum_{y=1}^b\sum_{d|gcd(\frac x k,\frac y k)}\mu(d) \\ &=\sum_{x=1}^{a/k}\sum_{y=1}^{b/k}\sum_{d|gcd(x,y)}\mu(d) \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(a/k,b/k)}\mu(d)\sum_{1\le x\le a/k\\ d|x}\sum_{1\le y\le b/k \\ d|y}1 \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(a/k,b/k)}\mu(d) \lfloor\frac{a}{kd}\rfloor \lfloor\frac{b}{kd}\rfloor \\ &=\sum_{d=1}^{min(A,B)}\mu(d) \lfloor\frac{A}{d}\rfloor \lfloor\frac{B}{d}\rfloor \end{aligned} $$

ZAP

$$ answer=\sum_{d=1}^{min(A,B)}\mu(d) \lfloor\frac{A}{d}\rfloor \lfloor\frac{B}{d}\rfloor $$

把 $\mu$ 筛出来，再做一个数论分块即可

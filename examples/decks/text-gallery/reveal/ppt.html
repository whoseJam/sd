Text Gallery

Visual walkthrough of sd.Text contracts.

→ / space to advance. Animated slides also use space to trigger.

defaults

Black "Hello" at center — fontSize 20, Times New Roman.

font-size

20 / 40 / 80 px stacked top→bottom — each line ~2× taller than the previous.

font-family

Top: Times New Roman (serif). Bottom: Arial (sans-serif). Same text, same size, visibly different glyph shapes.

anchor

Each red dot is at the same math y. Labels show what corner of the text box anchors to the dot for each constructor arg: (x,y) = bottom-left, (cx,cy) = vertical center on left edge, (centerX,centerY) = box center.

subtext-fill

Three "abcde" rows — top untouched (control), middle: chars 2-3 ("cd") red, bottom: chars 0-2 ("abc") blue. Only the highlighted range should be colored.

subtext-i-param

Three "ababab" rows with setSubtextFill("ab", red, i) at i=0,1,2 top→bottom. Expect the red pair to slide right as i grows — verifying the matchSubtext occurrence fix.

set-text-default

Press space — "1" transforms into "hello" with default mapping (chars fade in/out, no specific letter mapping).

set-text-mapping

Press space — "H" → "II" via explicit mapping {H: "II"}. The H should split into two I's, not fade.

set-text-insert

Press space — "1234" → "1+2+3+4" with mapping {1:"1", 2:"2", 3:"3", 4:"4"}. Digits should stay put; "+" glyphs should grow in between.

set-font-size-animated

Press space — fontSize 20 → 120 over 1.5s. The text bottom-left corner stays pinned to the red dot through the entire tween (math y must not drift).

set-font-family

Press space — Times New Roman morphs to Arial. Glyph outlines should interpolate continuously, not pop.

set-text-noop

Press space — setText("hello") on a Text already "hello" must be a true no-op: zero motion, zero flicker. (Locks in the early-return in setText.)

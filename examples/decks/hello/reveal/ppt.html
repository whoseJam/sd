Math Formulas

Inline math: the sum $\sum_{i=1}^{n} i = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$

Display math:

$$T(n) = T(n-1) + O(1) \implies T(n) = O(n)$$

$$T(n) = 2T\!\left(\frac{n}{2}\right) + O(n) \implies T(n) = O(n \log n)$$
